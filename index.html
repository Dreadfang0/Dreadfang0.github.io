Projects

H8FUL

A Retrofuturistic Top-down shooter in which the player character is looking for his head through a variety of different buildings, after it swapped with an animal head.

In this project I was mainly working on particle effects and shaders. I was also testing for bugs alongside working on audio effects and also made some minor features

Made with Unreal Engine 4.

Augur of Light

Augur of Light is a 1st person puzzle game, where you adventure in a Finnish island solving puzzles. During the first wave of covid-19 in Finland, we made this game as a school project about coping mechanisms for depression. We worked together with mental industry professionals.

I was mainly working on prototyping different puzzles and implementing them. I also worked on the particle effects.

The game can be found HERE

Made with Unity.

Valhalla Cannon

A project made on URHO3D with C++ for Android phones. Valhalla Cannon is an Angry birds like puzzle game where you shoot vikings from a cannon at different obstacles.

I was mainly working on UI as the URHO3D editor was not really that good at anything, everything had to be placed via code. I also worked on implementing puzzles.

Source code of Valhalla Cannon HERE Made with URHO3D.

Skeltal game

A project made for a summer course in 2019, where you shoot skeletons with a crossbow made out of bones in the wine cellar of a necromancer who happens to be your master. The game took part in Assembly Summer 2019's game dev competition.

I mainly worked on the AI but I also dabbled a little on the VFX side.
